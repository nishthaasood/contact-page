What is HelpKaro?

HelpKaro is a community-driven platform that connects donors with people in need. You can donate books, clothes, stationery, and money or request assistance if you need help.

How does this platform work?

Simply choose what you want to do—donate or request help. If you're donating, list the items or amount you'd like to contribute. If you're requesting, browse available donations or submit a request.

What items can I donate?

You can donate books, clothes, stationery, and money. Please ensure items are in good condition and usable.

Can I donate money?

Yes- We accept monetary donations to support individuals in need, fund community programs, provide education to those in need and improve donation logistics.

Is my monetary donation secure?

Yes, we use secure payment gateways to ensure your transaction is safe. You will also receive a receipt for your contribution through your mail id.

Do you provide pickup services for donations?

We offer pickup services in certain locations. Please check our service area or contact us to arrange a pickup. Or you can just drop your donation in a labelled box at our donation tents.

Do you accept corporate or bulk donations?

Yes- if your company or group wants to contribute, please contact us for special arrangements. We'll be more than happy to accept it.
